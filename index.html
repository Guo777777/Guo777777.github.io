I am a Master's student in Biomedical Engineering at Duke University, working in the Vision and Image Processing (VIP) Laboratory under Prof. Sina Farsiu. My research focuses on image processing, machine learning, and computer vision, with a particular interest in medical image analysis and image quality assessment (IQA). Previously, I received my B.E. in Biomedical Engineering (Class of Excellence) from Shenzhen University, where I developed deep learning models for echocardiogram landmark detection, CBCT orthodontic analysis, and semi-supervised tooth segmentation. I also conducted a research internship at Erasmus University Medical Center (Rotterdam) on GWAS with AI-derived brain MRI phenotypes.
