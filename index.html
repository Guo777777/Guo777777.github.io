I am a Master's student in Biomedical Engineering at Duke University, working in the Vision and Image Processing (VIP) Laboratory under Prof. Sina Farsiu. Previously, I received my B.E. in Biomedical Engineering (Class of Excellence) from Shenzhen University, where I developed deep learning models for echocardiogram landmark detection, CBCT orthodontic analysis, and semi-supervised tooth segmentation. My research interests revolve around the intersection of biomedical image analysis, computer vision, and multimodal large language models (MLLMs / VLMs), with a particular focus on image quality assessment (IQA). Specifically, I emphasize leveraging vision-language models to enhance the robustness and generalization capabilities of intelligent medical imaging systems.
